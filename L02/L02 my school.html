Al-Mazniayeh School

My school is Al-Mazniayeh School in Ein-Sara Street, Hebron.
I like the strategic way it is placed because it is close to my house,
which makes it easy for me to get there every day.
The number of students is around 700 wich consistsof girls only.

The school day starts at 8:00 in the morning and ends at 1:30 in the afternoon.
We study many subjects, such as Arabic, English, Math, Science, Islamic studies, Technology, and History.
Sometimes, we also have art classes and PE.
